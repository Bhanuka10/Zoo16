Food

Tailor your pet's diet to their needs with advice from your vet. Avoid human food to prevent digestive issues and maintain balance

Behavior

Exercise and stimulate. Use positive reinforcement, seek help if needed

Exercise

Exercise is vital for pet health. Daily walks, play, and toys keep them active. Tailor intensity to age, breed, health. Ensure mental and physical well-being

Grooming

"Maintain a grooming schedule for clean coats. Regular tooth brushing and professional dental cleanings, per vet's advice, safeguard against dental issues. Prioritize your pet's hygiene and oral health."
